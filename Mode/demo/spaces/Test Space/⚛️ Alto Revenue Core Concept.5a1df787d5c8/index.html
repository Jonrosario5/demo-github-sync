This report provides a comprehensive overview of Alto Pharmacy Revenue. The main table that powers these metrics contains daily snapshots of all orders in our production database starting in 2014 and continuing to the present day. This table is unaggregated and rich in dimensions and measures, such as segment and market region. This design empowers leadership to dig deeper into the data and discover their own insights without ever needing to touch code, thus freeing up analyst time to focus on more complex strategic and operational questions.
